My project is inspired by . . .
Speculative Design: can create artifacts, scenarios, or narratives that provoke critical thinking and discussion about the implications of AI on marginalized communities and societal structures.
Challenging Mental Models: exploration of alternative viewpoints and solutions to address systemic inequalities perpetuated by AI technologies.
Data Visualization: patterns, disparities, and impacts that might otherwise be overlooked.
Ethical Frameworks and Human Rights: analyzing how AI systems can either respect or infringe upon basic rights like privacy and fair treatment, especially among marginalized groups.
