Project Overview

Project Brief

This project, I am focusing on the intersection between artificial intelligence (AI) and social justice. The aim is to create a platform that serves as a informational platform for individuals interested in the social implications of AI.

Goals

This platform will highlight real-world examples where AI has impacted social justice, speciffically in relation to jobs, both positively and negatively.

Target Audience

  1. Activists
  2. Technologists.
  3. Concerned Citizens
