Project Overview

For this project, I am focusing on the intersection between artificial intelligence (AI) and social justice. The aim is to create a platform that serves as a informational platform for individuals interested in the social implications of AI.

This platform will highlight real-world examples where AI has impacted social justice, speciffically in relation to jobs, both positively and negatively.

The primary target audience for this project includes:
- Activists
- Technologists
- Concerned Citizens

Summary
