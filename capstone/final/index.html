AI systems are trained on historical data, which might reflect existing biases in job descriptions. If past job descriptions have been biased, the AI will likely perpetuate those biases?

Linguistic Bias
×

AI might perpetuate gender stereotypes by suggesting or favoring gendered terms that reinforce traditional roles (For example: associating men with leadership roles)?

Lack of Transparency
×

When AI systems make decisions without clear explanations, it becomes difficult to hold anyone accountable for the outcomes. If individuals affected by AI decisions cannot understand the rationale behind those decisions, they cannot effectively challenge or appeal them?

Misuse of AI Tools
×

Misuse of AI can lead to ethical breaches. Organizations might use AI as a scapegoat to deflect responsibility for biased or harmful decisions, claiming that the technology, not human oversight, is at fault?
