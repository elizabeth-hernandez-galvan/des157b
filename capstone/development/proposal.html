Project
Proposal

For my project, I will be working on the overall Davis Seal. I am planning on continuing this project as an Honors Thesis Project so I intend to work on a slice/part of the seal for this quarter and build off from there during Fall and Winter Quarters. Since I will be working on it as an Honors Project, I am not planning on working with a partner. Overall, my target audience would be geared towards adults. I will be focusing on college students and alumni as well as Davis residents and tourists. My project will serve as an informational means for those who are new to learn more about the city they are visiting or living in and for those who have been living here for years to learn more about the history behind Davis.

My project is inspired by a combination of Wheel of Fortune and Jeopardy where the user is able to spin the wheel to determine a value or in this case a topic and will have the option to learn or explore. The learn option will be more informational through the sharing of different stories from community members helping users to learn more about Davis. The explore option will be more of a trivia-based exploration in which each user will be able to find a new Davis location that is shown in the Seal or answer some sort of question/riddle based on the illustrations.

Given the remaining time constraint for this class, I am not sure I will be able to incorporate both options but if not feasible, I would like to incorporate both later on as I continue to work on my project.
